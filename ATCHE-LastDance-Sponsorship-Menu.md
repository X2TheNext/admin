# THE LAST DANCE — Sponsorship Menu
### A Juneteenth Block Party · Everyday People, U Street · June 19–20

*One night. One block. Free for everybody. The kind of crowd that remembers who showed up.*

This isn't a flyer logo — it's a spot inside the last great night at Everyday People, documented on video, lived in by hundreds of people, and talked about for months. Below are the ways a brand can show up.

---

## Presenting Sponsor — "Powered By"
*One slot only*

- "The Last Dance, powered by [Brand]" on every flyer, post, and the recap video's opening title card
- Branded loop on **all TVs/screens inside Everyday People** all night — your content, your colors, on repeat in front of the whole room
- Branded entrance signage + logo on the IG story frame used all night (skipping step & repeat — not enough lead time to produce one in a week)
- A dedicated shoutout from the stage/DJ at peak hour
- First credit in the tribute video, plus a 15–30 sec branded intro before it
- Premium placement at the activation table outside

**Price: $1,500–$3,000** (full wrap, one slot only)

---

## TV & Screen Takeover
*Sells separately or bundled with Presenting*

- Everyday People's TVs run a custom loop all night — product shots, a promo reel, a QR code to your page, whatever you send
- Easiest, lowest-lift way to be in front of every single person at the bar for hours
- Can run a different loop for "inside" vs. "outside the block party" if there are multiple screens

**Price: $400–$700** per night

---

## Print & Flyer Placement
- Logo on the official event flyer (digital + any printed posters around U Street)
- Co-branded flyer version for sponsors who want their own stack at the door / their storefront
- Inclusion in the IG/story graphics ATCHÉ + Everyday People push pre-event

**Price: $150–$300** flat

---

## Activation Table / Street Footprint
*The Cookies DC model — works for any product brand*

- A branded tent, table, or booth on the block
- Sampling, giveaways, or a codeword discount (like Cookies DC's **NOCAP = 25% off**) tied to your brand
- Direct face time with the crowd — not just a logo, an actual interaction
- Great for: cannabis/lifestyle brands, beverage brands, local fashion/jewelry, beauty brands

**Price: $400–$800** + product/samples

---

## Signature Drink / Menu Sponsorship
- A cocktail or mocktail named after your brand on the night's special menu
- Menu card/chalkboard credit: *"[Drink Name] — presented by [Brand]"*
- Optional: your product physically featured in the drink (spirits, mixers, energy drinks, etc.)

**Price: $250–$450** + product

---

## Social & Content Package
- Tagged post + story from **@atcheofficial** before, during, and after the event
- Inclusion in the recap video credits
- Circle members (creative women, content creators) can feature your activation organically in their own posts — built-in UGC
- Optional: your brand sends product for an on-camera "first impressions" moment during the night

**Price: $200–$400**, or bundled as an add-on

---

## Swag / Gift Bag Sponsor
- Item or sample included in any giveaway bags handed out at the door or activation tables
- Low cost, high goodwill — good entry point for smaller/local brands

**Price: $100–$200**, or product-in-kind

---

## Friend of the Block
*For U Street neighbors who just want to be part of it*

- Logo on the "thank you to the block" graphic and footer of event materials
- Mention in the recap video's closing credits
- Cross-promo: ATCHÉ tags your business in the U Street walking-tour content

**Price: No cash ask** — cross-promo / goodwill

---

## Why This Works

- **Built-in audience** — the Circle (creative women, 21+) plus Everyday People's regulars, all in one place
- **It's emotional** — this is a send-off night, the kind people show up for and remember. Brands attached to it get remembered too
- **It's documented** — full video + photo coverage means your placement doesn't end when the night does
- **Free entry = bigger crowd** — no cover means more people through the door, more eyes on every activation
